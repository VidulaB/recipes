Ingredients:
 - 1 cup butter
 - 1 cup flour
 - 1 cup sugar
 - 1 tsp cinnamon powder
 - 5-6 Apples

Directions:
  1. In a medium saucepan, melt butter and stir in flour, sugar and cinnamon powder for the topping
  2. Peel and cut apple into small pieces
  3. In a pan, spread apple evenly
  4. Spread topping all over it
  5. Bake in the oven at 350 degrees for 15-20 minutes
